About

Engineer. MBA. Builder.

Rigorous thinking meets hands-on execution. Both, always.

I am a Mechanical Engineer from NIT Kurukshetra who went to IIM Calcutta and spent the last 30 months inside some of India's most dynamic businesses — not advising them, but building inside them.

At Blinkit, I own location strategy and operational infrastructure across 2,000+ dark stores — the systems powering India's largest quick-commerce network at scale.

At Shopdeck, I managed commercial growth for 80+ D2C brands — generating ₹100 Cr+ in annualised business. I learned what it truly means to own a P&L, not just advise on one.

At Accenture S&C, I structured strategic procurement decisions under real stakes — should-cost modelling, vendor evaluation, and cross-stakeholder alignment.

"Strategy without execution is just creative writing. I care deeply about both."

Outside work, I think about how operational systems outlast their creators — and what separates a framework that looks good on a slide from one actually used in the field.
